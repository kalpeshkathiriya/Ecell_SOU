Who are we?

E-Cell IGNITE is a student body of Ignite, formed in 2014 with the aim to promote an entrepreneurial culture among the young minds of today's generation, and to encourage an entrepreneurial mindset in an aspiring individual to convert their "Drop of an Idea into an Ocean of Reality".

We believe that entrepreneurship can unlock India's latent inventive potential. It will not only push India to the forefront of the world but also help raise the quality of life in the country.

IDEATHON-22

An ideathon is an event where individuals or teams come together to generate and present new ideas. The format can vary, but typically includes brainstorming sessions, idea pitches, and feedback from judges or mentors. The goal is to generate innovative solutions to a specific challenge or problem. Winners of IDEATHON will be given pre-incubation support.

Art Competition

An Art competition event that connects entrepreneurship would showcase works that highlight innovation, creativity and risk-taking in art and business. Artists and entrepreneurs would have the opportunity to network, learn and purchase artworks. The event would exemplify the intersection of art and business and the mutual inspiration they provide.
