IDEATHON-22

 26 July 2022

An ideathon is an event where individuals or teams come together to generate and present new ideas. The format can vary, but typically includes brainstorming sessions, idea pitches, and feedback from judges or mentors. The goal is to generate innovative solutions to a specific challenge or problem. Ideathons can be focused on a particular industry or field, or can be more general. They can also be a fun and exciting way to foster creativity and collaboration. Winners of IDEATHON will be given pre-incubation support.

Sensitization Program

 29 Jun 2022

Sensitization on entrepreneurship cell is an event or program designed to educate and inform people about the opportunities and benefits of entrepreneurship.
