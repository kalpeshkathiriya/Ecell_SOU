E-Cell Entrepreneurship Cell is a non-profit student's organization dedicated to promoting the spirit of entrepreneurship among students throughout India.

E-Cell IGNITE, SOU aims to induce an entrepreneurial mindset into the students and air an innovative streak in them. We are here to water the Ideas in the bud and help them bloom into impactful endeavors through networking student enterprises from campus to incubators, seeding funds and angel investors to transform the newly proposed ideas into successful start-ups.

I-Scale Innovation Foundation (IGNITE),Non-Profit company, recognised as a Nodal Institute by Government of Gujarat (GoG) under Scheme for Assistance for Startups and Student Startup & Innovation Policy.

We organize various events, tasks, competitions, conclaves talks and our flagship event i.e. IDEATHON all by ourself. You will definitely hear about us somewhere or the other.

The main motto of E-cell is to pave and build the quality of entrepreneurship and team leading among students.

We have various teams like Content, Website, Technical, Social media, Designing, Event Management contains Hospitality, Discipline, Networking, On-Stage by working in which you can gain expertise of the same.

  1. Application: Submit an online application to the E-Cell, which may include basic information about yourself, your interests, and your experience.
  2. Selection: The E-Cell leadership will review the applications and select the candidates that are best suited for the organization. They may take into account factors such as your experience, interests, and goals.
  3. Orientation: Once you are selected, you will be required to attend an orientation session, where you will learn more about the E-Cell, its rules, and its events.
  4. Joining a team: After the orientation, you will be given the opportunity to join a team that aligns with your interests and skills.
  5. Participation: After joining a team, you will be expected to participate in the team's activities and events, and contribute to the E-Cell's mission and goals.
