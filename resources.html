Why Choose Us?

We are providing you the all resources and facilities to be needed to do a startup.
The following are the resources and facility is provided here at E-Cell IGNITE.

IPR Cell

An IPR Cell manages, protects, and promotes the intellectual property rights of an educational institution or a company. It creates awareness, identifies, documents, and commercializes intellectual property and provides legal assistance.

Conference Room

A conference room is a designated space in the IGNITE where meetings and presentations can be held. It typically includes a large table and chairs, as well as audio-visual equipment. It is smaller than Auditorium.

Auditorium

An auditorium is a large room or hall designed for holding public meetings, lectures, concerts, events or performances. It typically has tiered seating for audience members, a stage for performers, and audio-visual equipment.

IOT Lab

An IoT(Internet of Things) lab in a college is a facility where students and researchers can learn, research and develop IoT technologies, it's equipped with specialized hardware, software, and tools to prototype and test IoT solutions.

AI-ML Lab

An AI/ML lab in a college is a facility where students and researchers can learn, research and develop Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning technologies, it's equipped with powerful computers, software, and other specialized tools.

Robotics Lab

It is a facility where engineers and students design, develop and test robots and related technologies. Equipped with specialized tools, computers and software, it provides a space for innovation and collaboration in the field of robotics.

Maker's Lab

It provides students access to tools, materials, and equipment for creating, building, and prototyping projects, it's a space for innovation, experimentation, and learning across a wide range, from engineering to art and design.

3D Printer

It is a device that creates 3D objects by laying down successive layers of material, it is used to prototype the physical parts of a project and for experimentation in various fields. It is a valuable tool for students to bring their ideas to life.

Internet facility

we ensures that the entrepreneurs and start-ups have high-speed internet access to carry out their work efficiently, this includes access to cloud services, and other online tools. It can be wired or wireless, but it should be reliable and fast.

24x7 Access

It is important for businesses and entrepreneurs that require access to resources and facilities at any time, such as internet access, office space, and equipment.

Fully Air Conditioning

This is important for providing a comfortable working environment, especially in hot climates. It can also help maintain the equipment and materials in good conditions.

Projector

It allows you to give pitch their ideas to potential investors, customers, partners and to hold remote meetings and provide training sessions to the team.

Free Parking

it's a benefit for startup as it eliminates the need to pay for parking, it can also save time, reduce stress and make it more convenient to access the facility or location.

Pantry

It is a small kitchen area that provides a place to make beverages. It's a convenient way to provide food and drink options without the need to leave the building.

Drinking water

The important aspect of workplace health, providing drinking water can reduce the spread of disease, and promote hydration, which can improve health and productivity.
