What is E-Cell?

E-Cell

SOU aims to induce an entrepreneurial mindset into the students and air an innovative streak in them. We are here to water the ‘Ideas’ in the bud and help them bloom into impactful endeavors through networking student enterprises from campus to incubators, seeding funds and angel investors to transform the newly proposed ideas into successful start-ups.

VISION

The Incubation and E-Cell of Silver Oak University targets to help in the development of India's entrepreneurial ecosystem by enabling easy and efficient interaction between its major components spanning students, working professionals, aspiring entrepreneurs, mentors, angel investors and venture capital firms through initiatives like interactive sessions, events, conferences etc.

MISSION

The mission is to promote, sustain and practice the spirit of entrepreneurship and innovation among individuals by providing them a platform to convert their ideas into successful business. To inculcate the spirit of Entrepreneurship among young minds. E-Cell aims to convert feasible ideas into actual business propositions which will not only add value to individual but will also benefit the nation as a whole.

Why E-cell?

We believe that entrepreneurship can unlock India's latent inventive potential. It will not only push India to the forefront of the world but also help raise the quality of life in the country.
